Ravana Ella

Ravana Ella, situated in Ella, Sri Lanka, is a captivating waterfall that carries with it a sense of mystique and legend. Named after the mythical King Ravana from the ancient Indian epic, the Ramayana, this waterfall holds both natural beauty and historical significance. The cascading waters of Ravana Ella create a mesmerizing sight as they plunge from a height of approximately 25 meters into a tranquil pool below. Surrounded by lush greenery and rugged cliffs, the waterfall presents a picturesque scene that invites exploration and contemplation.
According to local folklore, Ravana Ella is believed to be one of the places where King Ravana hid Princess Sita after abducting her. It is said that Sita bathed in the pool at the base of the waterfall, adding to its mythical allure. The legend adds a sense of enchantment and wonder to the natural splendor of the site. Visiting Ravana Ella offers a serene and peaceful escape from the bustling city life. The sound of cascading water, coupled with the cool mist in the air, creates a soothing ambiance that rejuvenates the senses. It is a place to reconnect with nature, find inner peace, and appreciate the wonders of the natural world.
The journey to Ravana Ella is an adventure in itself. A short hike through the verdant forest leads you to the waterfall, offering glimpses of diverse flora and fauna along the way. The vibrant colors of tropical flowers and the chorus of bird songs add to the enchantment of the experience. For those seeking a bit of adventure, Ravana Ella provides an opportunity to swim in the natural pool at the base of the waterfall. Taking a refreshing dip in the cool waters while being surrounded by the natural beauty of the area is a rejuvenating experience that adds to the charm of the site.
Ravana Ella is also a popular spot for photography enthusiasts. The picturesque waterfall, surrounded by lush vegetation and dramatic cliffs, provides ample opportunities to capture stunning images and create lasting memories. Whether you are drawn to the legend and history associated with Ravana Ella or simply seek solace in nature's embrace, a visit to this captivating waterfall in Ella, Sri Lanka, promises an unforgettable experience. It is a place where myth and beauty intertwine, inviting you to immerse yourself in the serenity and wonder of the natural world.
