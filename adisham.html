Adisham Bungalow

Adisham Bungalow, located in Haputale, Sri Lanka, is a captivating colonial-era residence that carries with it an air of charm, history, and serenity. Nestled amidst rolling hills and lush green tea plantations, this iconic bungalow offers a glimpse into the country's colonial past. Originally built as a country retreat for Sir Thomas Villiers, a British planter, Adisham Bungalow later served as a monastery and is now open to the public as a museum and guesthouse. The bungalow's architecture reflects the colonial style of the time, with its red-brick walls, sloping roofs, and picturesque windows.
Stepping inside Adisham Bungalow is like stepping back in time. The interior showcases a collection of antique furniture, photographs, and memorabilia from the colonial era. As you wander through the rooms, you can imagine the genteel lifestyle of the British planters who once resided here. The tranquil surroundings of Adisham Bungalow add to its allure. The bungalow is enveloped by beautifully landscaped gardens, where colorful flowers and well-manicured lawns create a peaceful ambiance. The fragrance of blooming roses and the melodious songs of birds provide a serene backdrop to the experience.
One of the highlights of a visit to Adisham Bungalow is the opportunity to explore the on-site chapel and monastery. The chapel, adorned with stained glass windows and ornate woodwork, is a place of tranquility and spiritual reflection. The monastery, still inhabited by Benedictine monks, offers a glimpse into their simple yet meaningful way of life. For those seeking a retreat from the busyness of everyday life, Adisham Bungalow offers a serene and idyllic escape. The guesthouse provides comfortable accommodation amidst a tranquil setting, allowing visitors to relax and rejuvenate in the embrace of nature.
The bungalow's surroundings are also ideal for nature enthusiasts. A stroll through the surrounding tea plantations offers a chance to witness the intricate process of tea cultivation and plucking. The verdant hills and panoramic views provide ample opportunities for photography and immersing oneself in the natural beauty of the area. A visit to Adisham Bungalow is not complete without sampling their famous strawberry jam and scones. These homemade treats, made from locally sourced ingredients, are a testament to the bungalow's rich culinary heritage.
Adisham Bungalow in Haputale, Sri Lanka, is a destination that seamlessly weaves together history, natural beauty, and tranquility. It is a place to immerse oneself in the nostalgia of a bygone era, appreciate the splendor of the surroundings, and find solace in the serenity of the bungalow's gardens. Whether you are intrigued by colonial history, seeking a peaceful retreat, or simply captivated by the allure of tea country, Adisham Bungalow offers a memorable experience that transports you to a different time and place.
