WELCOME TO ELLA


Ella, a charming town in Sri Lanka's central highlands, captivates with its natural beauty. Surrounded by misty mountains, tea plantations, and rolling hills, it offers breathtaking vistas at every turn. Hike to Ella Rock or Little Adam's Peak for panoramic sunrises or sunsets, and visit tea estates to savor the flavors of Ceylon tea. With a laid-back atmosphere, friendly locals, and a range of outdoor activities, Ella is a haven for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Indulge in delicious cuisine, immerse in the tea culture, and let Ella's enchanting landscapes leave an everlasting impression.
