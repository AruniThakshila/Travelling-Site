Kithal Ella

Kithal Ella, situated in Ella, Sri Lanka, is a hidden gem nestled amidst lush greenery and tranquil surroundings. This lesser-known waterfall offers a serene and enchanting experience for those seeking a peaceful escape from the bustling city life. Tucked away in a remote corner, Kithal Ella reveals itself as a cascading beauty, gracefully flowing down from a height of approximately 20 meters. The waterfall is characterized by its gentle descent, creating a mesmerizing curtain of water that merges harmoniously with the natural landscape.
The journey to Kithal Ella is an adventure in itself. A short hike through a verdant forest leads you to this hidden gem. As you navigate the winding path, the sounds of chirping birds and rustling leaves accompany you, enhancing the sense of tranquility and seclusion. Arriving at Kithal Ella, you are greeted by the soothing sound of rushing water and the cool mist that permeates the air. The surrounding scenery is a feast for the eyes, with lush vegetation, vibrant flowers, and towering trees adding to the enchanting ambiance.
One of the unique features of Kithal Ella is the presence of natural pools at the base of the waterfall. These pools provide a refreshing oasis, inviting visitors to take a dip and immerse themselves in the crystal-clear waters. The invigorating experience of swimming beneath the cascading waterfalls is a true delight, rejuvenating both the body and soul. Kithal Ella offers a serene and peaceful atmosphere, making it an ideal spot for meditation, relaxation, and contemplation. The soothing sound of water and the gentle rustle of leaves create a calming backdrop, allowing visitors to connect with nature and find inner peace.
The untouched beauty and secluded location of Kithal Ella make it a hidden paradise for nature enthusiasts and photographers. The waterfall provides stunning photo opportunities, capturing the essence of its pristine surroundings and the natural symphony of water and greenery. While Kithal Ella may not be as well-known as some of the other waterfalls in Sri Lanka, its unspoiled charm and tranquility make it a hidden treasure worth exploring. It is a place to escape the crowds, revel in the serenity of nature, and create cherished memories in the heart of Ella, Sri Lanka.
